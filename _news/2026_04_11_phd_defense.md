It has been a long and rewarding journey, and I'm grateful to my advisors [Benjamin D. Leibowicz](https://sites.utexas.edu/leibowicz/) and [J. Eric Bickel](https://www.me.utexas.edu/people/faculty-directory/bickel), and my committee members John Hasenbein and Andrew Waxman for their support and guidance throughout.
