I host a weekly problem-solving seminar at UT Austin, inspired by the [HMC Putnam Seminar](https://catalog.hmc.edu/preview_course_nopop.php?catoid=20&coid=6774). We focus on combinatorics, probability, and programming puzzlesâ€”both for fun and as preparation for quantitative interviews.  

If you're interested in attending or learning more, feel free to reach out!  

Below, you'll find a collection of problems from past seminars (though not every session has a written record). Some solutions are also available as [blog posts](https://ccolombe12.github.io/blog/).
